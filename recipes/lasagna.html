Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) tomato pasta sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned; drain.
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce, parsley, and pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta, 1 1/2 cups of the mozzarella cheese, the Parmesan cheese, and the egg.
  5. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer 4 lasagna noodles, 1 cup of the cheese mixture, and 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with the meat sauce.
  6. Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese. Bake, uncovered, for 10 minutes more.
